Color Grounding Game

Point your camera at the desired color (or near) and hold it for a little bit./p>

You might need to give access to use your camera.

Why? You can use this as a grounding exercise. Like "Find a blue thing near you". It's up to you to not go back to the same thing again and again.

Disclaimer: No data is recorded or saved, everything is purely in your device.
